Fitness should be about celebrating what your body can do, not changing it to fit an ideal.

![Everybody Moves](/images/everybody-1.png)

## Redefining Fitness
All bodies are good bodies. Health looks different for everyone.

## Finding Joy in Movement
Try different activities until you find what you genuinely enjoy.

## Mental Health Benefits
Movement provides mood enhancement, stress relief, and improved confidence.

Your body is worthy of movement and care—celebrate it!
