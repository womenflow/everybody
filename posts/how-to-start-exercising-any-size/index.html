How to Start Exercising at Any Size

2026-07-08

Exercise is for everyone, regardless of size. Starting your fitness journey is about finding what works for your body and building consistency.

Start Where You Are

There’s no need to wait until you reach a certain size. You can exercise now, in your current body. Movement is beneficial for everyone.

Find Your Why

Exercise has many benefits beyond weight. You might enjoy the energy boost, stress relief, or social connection. Focus on how movement makes you feel.

Choose Activities You Enjoy

If you hate running, don’t run. Try walking, swimming, cycling, dancing, or yoga. You’re more likely to stick with activities you genuinely enjoy.

Go at Your Pace

Fitness is personal. Your pace is the right pace. Don’t compare your journey to others. Progress is progress.

Clothing That Fits

Wear comfortable, well-fitting exercise clothes. Don’t wait for the “perfect” outfit. When you feel good in what you’re wearing, you perform better.

Build Community

Exercise with others who share your values. Classes, groups, or online communities provide support and motivation.

Self-Compassion

There will be days you skip workouts. That’s normal. The key is getting back on track without judgment. You belong in every fitness space.

Always talk to a qualified healthcare provider before starting a new exercise routine — especially if you are pregnant, postpartum, recovering from injury, or managing a health condition.
